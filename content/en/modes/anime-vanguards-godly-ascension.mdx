## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is the anime vanguards godly ascension update?
The **anime vanguards godly ascension** update (Patch 13.5) is a massive content expansion that introduces six new units, seven unique Memorias, and seven Vanguard Familiars. It also features a complete overhaul of the cosmetic system, the introduction of Memoria Recycling via the Scrap Pirate NPC, the new Exclusive Shop, and the highly anticipated Summer Gamemode.

### How do I get the new units in anime vanguards godly ascension?
The new units can be acquired through multiple avenues. Lord (Fairy), Reaper (Scissors), and Cursed Doctor (Healer) are available via the Summer Banner and the Summer Gamemode. The secret unit, Raging Oni vs Wrath God, is earned through Worldlines. Finally, the exclusive Envoy of Destruction (Resistance) unit is unlocked by progressing through the seasonal Event Pass.

### How does the new Memoria Recycling system work?
Players can visit the Scrap Pirate NPC in the lobby to recycle duplicate or unwanted Memorias. Selling a Memoria awards you with one rarity-specific Memoria Scrap. These scraps can then be accumulated and traded back to the Scrap Pirate for specific Memorias that have rotated out of the active shop.

### What are the best rewards from the Summer Portals?
The most valuable rewards from the Summer Portals are the exclusive Vanguard Familiars, such as the Sun God's Mask and the Blazing Solstice. These companions provide massive stat multipliers to their designated units. Additionally, portals reward players with Summer Capsules, which contain Stat Chips, Iced Tea tokens, and rare evolution materials.
