## The Strategy for Success: Maximizing Affinity

One of the most common mistakes players make is bringing a team of diverse elements. According to community reports, the most effective way to clear high-floor counts is to focus your roster on a single element.

The "Affinity" card is your best friend in this mode. It provides massive buffs to units of a specific element. By bringing only one element, you ensure that the Affinity buff is always applied to your strongest units rather than being randomized across a scattered team.

### Why Element Consistency Matters
- **Affinity Buffs:** Stacking up to three times, it grants a total of +90% damage and -90% SPA.
- **Reliability:** You never have to worry about your "meta" unit missing out on the buff because of an RNG element roll.
- **DPS Potential:** Even lower-tier units of the same element often outperform high-tier mixed-element teams due to these massive percentage boosts.

## Navigating Elite Floors and Mechanics

Elite floors are where runs often end for unprepared players. These stages feature enemies with specific environmental or status-based modifiers. You can identify the behavior of an elite enemy by the color of their aura.

### Elite Enemy Color Guide
- **Red:** Gains movement speed upon nearby enemy death.
- **Orange:** Doubles speed after taking a large burst of damage.
- **Yellow:** Provides a speed aura to nearby enemies.
- **Green:** Gains damage reduction based on the number of alive enemies.
- **Blue:** Invulnerable until linked units are defeated.
- **Purple:** Cycles between invulnerability and vulnerability windows.

Strategic play is required here. For example, if you encounter a Green-aura elite, focus on thinning the herd before dumping your damage abilities into the primary target.

## Advanced Tips for Efficient Farming

If you want to speed up your progression, consider the following expert-level advice:

1. **Wait for Autoplay:** Do not rely on autoplay until you have secured all three stacks of Affinity and a strong set of character cards. Attempting to auto-play too early often leads to failed runs.
2. **Stitches' Special:** Keep an eye out for Stitches. In this mode, he can mark unit cards with special passives, such as bypassing damage reduction or providing immunity to status effects. These can be the difference between a successful run and a wipe.
3. **Ascension Management:** Once you hit Level 100 on the Level Tree, you can begin Ascending. While this resets your tree, it allows you to tackle higher-difficulty runs, yielding better rewards. Only ascend when your team is consistently clearing the base mode with ease.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the fastest way to farm in Anime Vanguards Odyssey?
Focus on obtaining the "Gold Thread" and "Shop Cleaner" modifiers early in your run. This maximizes your coin gain, allowing you to purchase high-tier cards and reroll the shop more frequently to hunt for mythic items.

### Should I prioritize Elite rooms or Normal rooms?
Prioritize Elite rooms whenever your team is strong enough. They offer significantly higher rewards and are the only way to obtain the exclusive unit cards needed to optimize your character's performance.

### How do I unlock the best character cards?
You must increase your character's Proficiency Rank. As you play, you unlock more slots for character-specific cards. Once you have filled the basic card slots, the shop will prioritize offering rare character cards, which is the most reliable way to build a top-tier loadout.

### Is it better to play solo or in a group?
While the mode supports both, solo play allows you to control the route and resource management perfectly. If you are struggling, a coordinated group can help, but solo play is generally preferred for efficient farming once you understand the mechanics.
