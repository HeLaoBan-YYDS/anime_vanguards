## Finding the Lost Zoro NPC

Once you finish the bounty and return to the lobby, a system message will pop up stating: *"Bounty Hunter has gotten lost and needs help being found."* 

At this point, you will notice that the Zoro unit you had equipped has completely disappeared from your inventory. Do not panic! This step is crucial in the **anime vanguards how to evolve zoro** questline. You must now track him down in Story Mode to get him back and secure your evolution item.

### Steps to Retrieve Zoro:
1. Enter any act of the new story mode (Stage 14, Act 1, Act 2, or Act 3).
2. Upon loading in, a notification will read: *"Someone looks lost."*
3. Thoroughly search the map for the **Bounty Hunter (Zoro) NPC**. 
4. His spawn location is randomized for each player, so look behind structures, near corners, and along the edges of the map.
5. Once you locate him, walk up and click **Help**.

By helping him, you will receive the **Foreign Drink** (his essential evolution item), and Zoro will automatically return to your character inventory.

## Zoro's Passive Abilities Explained

What makes the King of Hell form a true "burn demon" are his three unique passives. These passives allow him to scale his damage rapidly while generating extra income and resources for your team.

### 1. Legendary Bounty Hunter
* **Effect:** When Zoro attacks a burning enemy, he inflicts the **Wanted** status. You gain an additional **50 Yen** for every takedown on a Wanted enemy.
* **Bonus:** This passive triples the rewards you receive from Bounty Missions and doubles the Rift Shards gained from completing Rifts.

### 2. Hunter of Pirates
* **Effect:** Zoro's attacks inflict **Bleed** (dealing 50% of his base damage over 10 seconds) and **Burn** (dealing 20% of his damage over 10 seconds).
* **Bonus:** He deals 50% more damage to Wanted targets. Additionally, if an enemy within his range gets healed, Zoro instantly deals 100% of his damage to them and inflicts **Rupture** (has a 10-second cooldown).

### 3. King of Hell
* **Effect:** When attacking an enemy that is already burning from Zoro's attacks, he applies an additional burn stack for every existing stack on that target (up to a maximum of 4 stacks).
* **Bonus:** Zoro receives a damage buff of **+0.5x damage per burn stack** on his current target. This damage buff resets as soon as he targets a new enemy.

### How do I start the Zoro evolution quest in Anime Vanguards?
To start, open your Quest menu and locate the "King of Hell Evolution Quest." You must first complete a bounty mission from the Bounty Hunter NPC in the Story Event lobby while having Zoro equipped.

This is a scripted part of the evolution questline. After completing your first bounty, a message will pop up saying Zoro has gotten lost. You must enter Stage 14 (Act 1, 2, or 3) in Story Mode, find the Zoro NPC hidden on the map, and help him to get him back.

### In anime vanguards how to evolve zoro, where does the Bounty Hunter NPC spawn?
During the "lost" phase of the quest, the Bounty Hunter NPC spawns randomly on the map of any Stage 14 Story Mode act. You must explore the map, find him, and interact with him to receive the Foreign Drink and return him to your inventory.

### Can I get Zoro's evolution materials without farming Imprisoned Island?
Yes. While the Weather Rod, Strings, and Food Platters drop from the Imprisoned Island X Stage, you can also use the game's Synthesize system to craft them by converting other evolution items that you do not need.
