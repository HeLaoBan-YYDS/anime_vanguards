## Step-by-Step: How to Solve the Compass Pose

The first major hurdle players face is correctly utilizing the Compass Pose. Many players mistakenly equip this item on the wrong unit. According to [community reports](https://www.roblox.com/games/14022863479/Anime-Vanguards), you must equip the Compass Pose specifically onto a Navigator unit—specifically Nami—to trigger the next phase of the questline.

### The Treasure Map Hunt
Once the Compass Pose is equipped, your next objective is to locate the Treasure Map. This item is found in the Flower Country story stage. 

1.  **Identify the Stage:** Look for the act in Flower Country that features a golden highlight.
2.  **Farm the Map:** Engage in that act; the Treasure Map has a 10% drop rate.
3.  **Purchase Tools:** Visit the Gold Shop and buy the "Item Finder." You cannot see the hidden dig location without it.
4.  **Locate the Prize:** Head to Story Stage 2 (Sand Village), Act 4. Follow the indicator provided by the Item Finder to dig up the Left Half of the Sun.

## Navigating the Pirate Dynasty and Boss Rush

After securing the first half of the sun, you must head to the Pirate Dynasty Limited Mode. This stage requires a mix of combat skill and tactical survival.

Player experience suggests that using a unit with high range, such as Hill Killer, makes managing the points and the subsequent boss battles significantly easier. Remember, you do not need to defeat Kaido; simply survive the encounter to obtain the drumstick necessary for the final, most difficult segment of the quest.

## Mastering the Drum Rhythm Challenge

The final piece of the puzzle involves the Floating Island Boss Rush. This is where many players struggle, as it requires precise timing.

### Rhythm Challenge Tips
*   **Audio Settings:** Increase your SFX volume to at least 50% and mute the game music to hear the drum beats clearly.
*   **The Timing:** Locate the drums near the giant skull. You must press 'E' exactly when the sound triggers. You need to hit this timing three times perfectly.
*   **Zunesha's Appearance:** If done correctly, you will receive a notification that the drums are echoing. Zunesha will appear, signaling that you have succeeded.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why can't I see the dig location in Sand Village?**
A: You must purchase the Item Finder from the Gold Shop. Without this tool, the indicator will not appear on your HUD, making it impossible to **solve the compass pose** quest requirements.

**Q: Do I need to beat Kaido to get the drumstick?**
A: No, you do not need to defeat him. In fact, you are intended to lose the encounter to receive the drumstick item, which is a common point of confusion for players trying to **solve the compass pose** questline.

**Q: Is it possible to fail the rhythm challenge?**
A: Yes. If you miss the timing for the drums, Zunesha will not appear. You must practice the timing by listening to the sound cues and clicking precisely when the visual marker aligns.

**Q: What happens when I finally evolve my unit?**
A: You will unlock the Gear 5 form of your unit, along with the associated Memoria. This transformation is a significant power boost and essential for endgame content in Anime Vanguards.
