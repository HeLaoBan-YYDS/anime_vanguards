## Step 1: The Compass Pose and the Navigator
The quest line starts by completing a single bounty stage. Once you finish this, you will receive the Compass Pose. According to community reports, this item is useless until equipped correctly. You must assign the Compass Pose to your Navigator unit (Nami) to trigger the next phase of the quest. Without this specific setup, you will not be able to track the treasure map required for the first half of the sun.

## Step 2: Locating the Treasure Map
After equipping the Compass Pose, head over to the Flower Country map. You are looking for a specific act—either Act 1, 2, or 3—that displays a distinct golden border. This signifies that the stage contains the hidden map.

*   **Drop Rate:** The map has a 10% drop chance.
*   **Pro Tip:** Keep grinding the golden-bordered act until the item drops. It may take several attempts, so patience is key.

Once you obtain the treasure map, inspect it in your inventory. You will notice numerical hints (e.g., "2-4"). These correlate to the Story Map stage and act number. Before heading there, visit the gold shop to purchase the "Item Finder." You cannot see the hidden dig site without this tool equipped.

## Step 4: The Boss Rush and the Drums of Liberation
The final hurdle in learning **anime vanguards how to evolve luffy** is the boss rush stage. This is widely considered the most difficult part of the quest. You will need to interact with the environment to trigger the Gear 5 transformation.

1.  **Mute the Music:** Increase your SFX volume to around 50% so you can hear the cues clearly.
2.  **Find the Drums:** Locate the drums on the staircase area of the map.
3.  **The Timing Minigame:** You must press 'E' exactly when the drum sound hits the circle indicator. You need to do this three times perfectly.
4.  **Survival:** Once you succeed, Zunesha will appear. Note that enemies will become significantly faster and stronger.

If you are struggling to survive this phase, ensure your units are maxed out before you trigger the drum sequence. If you still find yourself leaking enemies, bringing a unit like Ichigo is highly recommended for his crowd control abilities.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I start the quest for Gear 5 Luffy?**
You can find the "Elastic Captain Vanguard" quest in the special tab of your quest menu. Simply complete a bounty stage to get the Compass Pose and get started.

**Why can't I see the dig site for the sun half?**
You must purchase the "Item Finder" from the gold shop. Without this equipped, the hidden indicators will not appear on the map, making it impossible to progress in your quest to learn **anime vanguards how to evolve luffy**.

**What is the best way to handle the boss rush?**
The best strategy is to focus on farming yen early to max out your units before triggering the drums. Alternatively, bring a unit with a "pull-back" effect, like Ichigo, to manage leaks when the enemies speed up.

**Do I need to beat Kaido in the Pirate Dynasty?**
No, you actually need to lose to Kaido in that specific stage to obtain the drumstick item. Beating him is not required for this part of the quest, so don't worry if you get overwhelmed!
