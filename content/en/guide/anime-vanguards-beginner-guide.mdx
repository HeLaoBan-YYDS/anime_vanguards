## Getting Started: The Initial Progression Path

When you first spawn into the lobby, it is tempting to jump straight into the story mode. However, experienced players recommend completing the tutorial and immediately focusing on your starter units. You will receive Nami and Zoro, both of which are essential for your early-game success. 

Zoro is particularly valuable because his evolution is straightforward. By utilizing the hat item provided early on, you can evolve him into a high-damage unit that only costs $600 to place, making him a staple for almost every stage you will face.

## The Power of the Leveling Loop

Efficiency is the name of the game in Anime Vanguards. Instead of aimlessly playing through long, difficult stages, you should focus on the "leveling loop." This strategy involves entering a stage with short waves, placing a strong carry unit, and resetting the run after wave 10.

According to community reports, this loop is the fastest way to farm XP, gold, and gems. Because the game rewards you for the number of completed runs rather than the duration of the match, quick resets are superior to long, drawn-out sessions.

### Why the Leveling Loop Works
*   **Faster XP Gains:** Completing more runs in less time accelerates your account level.
*   **Consistent Drops:** You accumulate gems and gold at a much faster rate.
*   **Reduced Difficulty:** Focusing on the first 10 waves allows you to use minimal resources while maximizing output.

## Mastering Random Events: The Griffith Strategy

One of the most underrated mechanics in the game is the "Random Stage Event." By navigating to your settings and enabling this feature, you unlock the potential to trigger the Griffith Eclipse event during stages 1 through 10.

Player experience suggests that when this event triggers, you should remove all units from your team and allow the event to complete. This is the primary method for obtaining Griffith. Once you have two Griffith units, you can combine them into a "Struggler Mythic," which is a top-tier mid-game unit that will carry you through significantly harder content.

## Pro-Tips for Long-Term Success

To maintain your edge, keep these community-vetted tips in mind:
1.  **Don't Evolve Everything:** Some units, like Jiraiya, lose their utility or become too expensive if evolved too early. Keep them in their base form until you have the economy to support them.
2.  **Focus on Traits:** Once you have a steady supply of rerolls, prioritize getting "Divinity" or "Jack of All" traits on your primary carry units.
3.  **Use Your Alts:** If you have the capability, managing multiple accounts can significantly speed up your resource gathering, as you can funnel materials to your main account.
4.  **Watch the Event Calendar:** The game frequently drops new content. Always check if a new event offers better XP-per-hour than your current loop.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**What is the best way to use this anime vanguards beginner guide?**
The best way to use this **anime vanguards beginner guide** is to follow the steps in order: start with the tutorial, focus on evolving Zoro, and then immediately begin the leveling loop to build your economy.

**How do I get essence stones quickly?**
Essence stones are best acquired through completing challenges, participating in event shops, and clearing legend stages. Make sure to claim all active codes, as they often provide free bundles of these stones.

**Should I focus on summoning or evolving first?**
Always prioritize evolving your current units over mass summoning. A fully evolved starter unit like Zoro or Nami will perform better than a base-level mythic unit in the early stages of the game.

**Is it possible to AFK farm in Anime Vanguards?**
Yes, many players use cloud-based solutions or simple macro scripts to automate the leveling loop. However, ensure you are in a safe environment and have a stable connection to avoid losing progress during long AFK sessions.
