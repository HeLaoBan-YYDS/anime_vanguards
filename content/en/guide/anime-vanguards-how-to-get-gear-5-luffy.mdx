## Step-by-Step Guide: The Cog 5th Questline

Once you have your Pirate Luffy ready, navigate to your "Special Tab" in the menu. You should see the **Elastic Captain Vanguard Quest**. This is your roadmap for **anime vanguards how to get gear 5 luffy**.

### Step 1: The Compass Pose
The first step is relatively straightforward. You must defeat a single **Bounty Stage**. It doesn't matter which difficulty or map you choose, as long as it is a designated Bounty mission. Upon completion, you will receive a familiar called the **Compass Pose**.

**Important Note:** The Compass Pose is a "navigator" item. To proceed, you must equip this familiar on a Nami unit (the Navigator). The quest description hints that only a navigator can understand the directions provided by the pose. Once equipped, the next stage of the quest unlocks.

### Step 2: Finding the Treasure Map
With your Navigator ready, head over to the **Flower Country** story map. Look closely at the Act selection screen. One of the acts (Act 1, 2, or 3) will have a distinct **golden lining** around its border. This indicates the current location of the treasure.

*   **Drop Chance:** The Treasure Map has a 10% drop rate from this specific stage.
*   **Action:** Play the stage repeatedly until the map drops.

### Step 3: Digging for the Left Half of the Sun
Once the map is in your inventory, inspect it. You will see coordinates—specifically, the numbers "2" and "4." This refers to **Stage 2 (Sent Village), Act 4 (Sosora Shell)**. 

Before heading there, visit the **Gold Shop** and purchase the **Item Finder**. This tool is mandatory; without it, you cannot see the digging indicator. Enter Sent Village Act 4, follow the golden arrow on your screen, and interact with the ground to dig. You will receive the **Left Half of the Sun**.

### Step 4: The Drumstick and the Pirate Dynasty
The next phase takes you to the **Pirate Dynasty** map. This is a combat-heavy step where you must complete the following:
1.  Capture all four points on the map as you would in a normal match.
2.  Defeat the Zoro boss spawn. (Community reports suggest using units with high range, like Hillkiller, to out-range his attacks).
3.  **Lose to Kaido:** After Zoro falls, Kaido will appear. You do not need to beat him here. In fact, you must let him defeat you to trigger the next quest item.

After losing to Kaido, you will receive the **Drumstick**, accompanied by a message stating that "a drum begins to call from the floating island."

## The Grand Finale: Boss Rush and Zunesha

The final and most difficult step in learning **anime vanguards how to get gear 5 luffy** takes place in the **Boss Rush** stage. This requires precision and a specific loadout to survive the increased difficulty.

### The Rhythm Mini-Game
Before entering, go to your settings and **mute the music** while keeping your **SFX volume at 50% or higher**. This is crucial for hearing the drum cues.
1.  Enter the Boss Rush stage.
2.  Locate the staircase near the back of the map where the drums are located.
3.  A circle will appear on your screen. You must press "E" (or the interact button) exactly when the drum sound plays.
4.  You must successfully time this **three times**.

If done correctly, a notification will appear: "The drums echo across the island." You will see the massive elephant, **Zunesha**, appear in the background.

### Surviving the Onslaught
Once Zunesha appears, the enemies receive a massive buff to speed and health. This is where many players fail. Player experience suggests bringing **Ichigo (Hillkiller)** for this part. Ichigo’s ability to pull units back when they reach your base (after his revive) is a lifesaver. 

Wait for the cutscene to end. Your unit will automatically transform into Gear 5 Luffy. In this "God Mode," you will have massively increased stats. Use this power to defeat the final Kaido boss. Once he is defeated, you will receive the **Right Half of the Sun**.

### Passives and Abilities
*   **King of Pirates:** Grants all Pirate category units a 30% damage buff when they are within range. It also marks enemies with a "Wanted" status, granting 30% more yen on kill.
*   **Sun God's Transformation:** This is the core mechanic. While active, Luffy's damage increases by 50% for every ability currently on cooldown. He also gains a stacking 1% damage buff every second (up to 100%).

## Tips for a Smooth Quest Run

To ensure you don't waste time or resources, keep these community-vetted tips in mind:

1.  **Disable Unit Cutscenes:** During the Boss Rush phase, cutscenes can distract you from the rhythm game. Turn them off in the settings.
2.  **Farm Essence Early:** Don't wait until you have the Sun items to start farming Rainbow and Red essence. These have low drop rates in standard play.
3.  **The Ichigo Strategy:** As mentioned, Ichigo is almost mandatory for the Zunesha phase because of his "pullback" mechanic. If you don't have Ichigo, make sure you have a maxed-out slow/stun unit like Flower Magician to handle the speed-buffed enemies.
4.  **Check the Golden Lining:** The Golden Lining in Flower Country resets. If you don't get the map drop and the day rolls over, check which Act is glowing again.
5.  **Memoria Unlock:** You automatically receive the "He Laughed" Memoria upon evolving. This specialty item further boosts your damage against "Repulsed" enemies.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get the quest to show up in Anime Vanguards?
To see the Cog 5th questline, you must have the **Elastic Captain (Pirate)** unit in your inventory. Once you have the unit, check the "Special" tab in your quest menu. If it still doesn't appear, ensure your game is updated to version 13.0 or higher.

### Can I get Gear 5 Luffy without Nami?
While you don't need Nami for the final fight, she is required for the first step. The **Compass Pose** familiar must be equipped on a Navigator unit (Nami) to reveal the location of the Treasure Map. Without her, you cannot progress past the first stage of the quest.

### What is the drop rate for the Treasure Map?
The Treasure Map has a **10% drop rate**. You must play the specific Act in Flower Country that has the golden lining around it. If you play an Act without the golden lining, the map will not drop regardless of how many times you clear it.

### Why did I lose the Drumstick after Kaido?
You actually *want* to lose to Kaido in the Pirate Dynasty stage. If you defeat him, you won't get the item. The quest triggers the reward specifically when the "Drums of Liberation" are needed to revive the captain, which happens after a defeat.
