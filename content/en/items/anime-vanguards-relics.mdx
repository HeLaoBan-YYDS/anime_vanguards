The secret to reaching the 600,000 currency per hour mark lies in the internal clock of the game's quests. Many players fail to realize that quests operate on a 15-wave cycle: a 5-wave clash followed by a 10-wave cooldown before the next showdown appears.

To maximize your gains, you must complete the first batch of quests by Wave 20 or 21. If you finish the quest at exactly Wave 20, the next quest becomes available at Wave 36. Completing this second quest before Wave 40 triggers a massive currency multiplier for that milestone. 

### The "Nth Wave" Strategy

Player experience indicates that timing your completions to the 19th or 29th wave is ideal. If you are too slow and finish a quest at Wave 22, the next one won't appear until Wave 38, leaving you almost no time to finish it before the Wave 40 payout.
