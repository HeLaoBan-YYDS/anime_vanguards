## Who is Yomomata (Captain) in Anime Vanguards?

Yomomata (Captain), frequently referred to by players as **anime vanguards yamamoto**, is a Secret-tier unit that specializes in dealing massive fire-based damage-over-time. Originally released during the Bleach-themed update, this unit has remained a staple for players who love summoning mechanics and burn builds. 

What makes him unique is his dual identity as both a summoner and a DOT dealer. His primary passive allows him to raise fallen enemies as skeleton soldiers that march down the track, dealing damage that scales directly off his base attack power. 

With the addition of his exclusive Memoria and Vanguard Familiar upgrades, his skill ceiling has reached astronomical heights. When properly optimized, he is widely considered by the community to be one of the highest effective DPS units in the entire game.

## Late-Game Meta Strategy and Team Synergies

While his on-paper damage is incredibly high, playing him effectively in late-game content requires careful planning. Player experiences highlight a major drawback: the skeleton summons are incredibly slow. On larger maps, fast-moving bosses can easily outrun the skeletons, rendering their melee attacks useless.

To combat this, players recommend using his "North" active skill to halt the skeletons near key choke points. Once a boss walks into the cluster, detonate them using the Vanguard Familiar ability for massive burst damage.

Additionally, he should always be paired with DOT replication and amplification units. Units like Hibana or Kafka (The Manipulator) can copy his intense burn stacks and spread them to surrounding enemies. This combo can easily push your team's effective DPS into the tens of millions, making quick work of even the toughest portal and rift bosses.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get the Vanguard Familiar for anime vanguards yamamoto?
You must play Odyssey mode and raise your unit proficiency to ranks 5 and 10. Once you reach these milestones, you can claim the "Burning Blade" Vanguard Familiar, which unlocks powerful detonation mechanics and stat boosts.

### What is the difference between Yomomata (Captain) and Yamamoto?
They are the same character. "Yomomata (Captain)" is the in-game name used in Anime Vanguards to avoid copyright issues, while players commonly refer to him as Yamamoto due to his inspiration from the Bleach anime series.

### Is the anime vanguards yamamoto unit good for infinite mode?
Yes, he is highly effective in infinite mode due to his infinite-stacking burn damage and status immunity. However, because his skeleton summons are slow, you should pair him with slow or stun units like Gojo to keep enemies within his attack zone.

### What is the best trait to roll on this unit?
The absolute best traits for him are Monarch and Solar. Monarch provides the best overall stat increases, while Solar boosts his fire-based DOT output, making his intense burn stacks deal significantly more damage over time.
