## How to Evolve Anime Vanguards Luffy: The Cog 5th Questline

To evolve your Elastic Captain (Pirate) into the legendary Cog 5th form, you cannot simply rely on standard evolution materials. Instead, you must complete a highly specific, multi-step questline found under your "Special" quest tab. This questline is titled the **Elastic Captain Vanguard Quest**. 

Completing this journey rewards you with the two essential evolution items: the **Left Half of the Sun** and the **Right Half of the Sun**. Below is the complete step-by-step walkthrough based on community reports and successful player strategies.

### Step 1: Defeat a Bounty Stage & Equip the Compass Pose
Your journey begins by heading to your special quest tab and tracking the active quest. 
* **Objective:** Complete any singular Bounty Stage.
* **Reward:** You will receive a unique familiar called the **Compass Pose**.
* **Action Required:** Open your inventory and equip the Compass Pose familiar onto **Nami** (Navigator). The item's passive description explicitly states that it requires a navigator to understand.

### Step 2: Hunt for the Treasure Map in Flower Country
Once Nami is equipped with the familiar, a new path opens up in the Flower Country.
* **Objective:** Navigate to the Flower Country story map. Look closely at the Act selection screen; one of the Acts (Act 1, 2, or 3) will feature a distinct golden lining.
* **Action Required:** Select and play this golden Act. The **Treasure Map** has a 10% drop chance upon completion. You may need to run this stage multiple times until the map drops.

### Step 3: Decode the Map & Dig for the Left Half of the Sun
Once you obtain the Treasure Map, open your inventory and inspect it. You will see the numbers **2** and **4** written on it, along with a hint about buying tools.
* **Objective:** Go to the Gold Shop in the lobby and purchase the **Item Finder** tool.
* **Action Required:** Enter Stage 2, Act 4 (**Sent Village: Sosora Shell**). Because you have the Item Finder equipped, a digging indicator will appear on your screen once you load into the map. Follow the indicator to the designated dig site, interact with it, and claim the **Left Half of the Sun**.

### Step 4: Face Zoro and Kaido in Pirate Dynasty
With the first half of the sun secured, your quest directs you toward the Pirate Dynasty stage.
* **Objective:** Play through the Pirate Dynasty stage normally. You must capture all four control points on the map.
* **Action Required:** Once all four points are captured, a Zoro boss will spawn. Defeat Zoro (using high-range units like Hill Killer makes this fight trivial). Immediately after Zoro falls, an unusual Kaido boss will spawn.
* **Crucial Tip:** You do **not** need to defeat Kaido here. Simply let Kaido defeat you. Upon losing the match, you will receive the **Drumstick** item, accompanied by a prompt stating that drums are calling from the floating island.

### Step 5: The Drums of Liberation & The Right Half of the Sun
This is widely considered by the community to be the most challenging phase of unlocking the **anime vanguards luffy** evolution. Before launching this stage, go to your settings, mute your in-game music, and turn your Sound Effects (SFX) volume up to at least 50%.
* **Objective:** Enter the **Boss Rush** stage. 
* **Action Required:** Locate the stone staircase on the map and walk up to find a set of physical drums. You must interact with the drums and time your button presses (pressing 'E') perfectly with the shrinking circle on your screen. You must hit this rhythmic QTE successfully three times.
* **The Challenge:** Once timed correctly, a notification will read: *"The drums echo across the island."* At this moment, Zunesha (the giant elephant) will appear in the background. Enemies will immediately receive a massive speed and health buff, making them incredibly difficult to stop.
* **Survival Strategy:** Player experience suggests placing your farms early and maxing out a few high-tier defensive units before attempting the drum QTE. Alternatively, bringing **Ichigo** is highly recommended. Ichigo's pullback mechanic upon stock-leak can save you from an instant defeat.
* **The Transformation:** If you survive long enough for Zunesha's cutscene to end, your unit will temporarily transform into an ultra-powerful Gear 5 Luffy. Use this temporary form to easily crush Kaido, which rewards you with the **Right Half of the Sun**.

## Passive Abilities & Memoria Specialty

What truly sets the evolved **anime vanguards luffy** apart from other damage-dealers is his unique passive kit. His abilities provide massive buffs to fellow Pirate units, inflict unique debuffs on enemies, and grant status immunity.

### Core Unit Passives
* **King of Pirates:** All Pirate category units gain a flat 30% damage increase while standing within this unit's range. Additionally, any enemy in range that experiences a *Repulse* effect is permanently marked as *Wanted*. Wanted enemies yield 30% more yen upon death and take an additional 30% damage from Pirate units.
* **Right Armaments:** This unit is completely immune to all incoming Status Effects. If an enemy attempts to inflict a status effect, they are instantly *Repulsed*, and their SPA is reduced by 5%. Furthermore, this passive decreases the deployment and upgrade cost of all Pirate allies by 2% per upgrade level.
* **Sun God's Transformation:** Grants total immunity to all status effects and debuffs. While active, the unit's damage is buffed by 50% for every ability currently on cooldown. It also gains a ramping 1% damage buff per second (capping at 100%). Upon deactivation, this ramping buff is retained for 120 seconds. Finally, it inspires all allies with *Joy*, causing their attacks to randomly inflict either a 30% slow or a damage-over-time status effect.

### Memoria Specialty Passives ("He Laughed")
* **Moment of Opportunity:** Deals 35% more damage when attacking any enemy currently under a *Repulse* lockout.
* **Loveable Captain (Locked Specialty):** After this unit uses its meter, it generates future meter 30% faster for 20 seconds. Non-enhanced skills generate 10% meter per use, while enhanced skills apply *Rupture*.
* **The Banging Drums (Locked Specialty):** Automatically inflicts *Repulse* on any enemy within range the exact moment they exit their *Repulse* lockout.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I get the anime vanguards luffy unit in the game?
To obtain the **anime vanguards luffy** unit, you must first summon his base version, Elastic Captain, through the standard summon gates. From there, you must evolve him into Elastic Captain (Pirate) using a Straw Hat and various basic essences. Finally, you can unlock his ultimate Cog 5th form by completing the special Cog 5 Evolution Questline.

### Why won't my Compass Pose familiar work on other units?
The Compass Pose familiar is designed with a specific passive that requires a navigator to decipher. You must equip it on **Nami** (Navigator) in your inventory to trigger the next step of the Cog 5th questline.

### What is the best strategy for the Boss Rush drum stage?
According to community reports, the easiest way to clear the Boss Rush drum QTE is to max out your economy farms and place strong defensive units before touching the drums. Utilizing **Ichigo** is highly recommended because his pullback mechanic serves as a safety net against the incredibly fast enemies spawned by Zunesha. Additionally, remember to disable unit cutscenes in your settings to prevent unnecessary delays.

### Do upgrades carry over when switching between Cog 4th and Cog 5th?
Yes! When you use the **Cog 4th** active skill to de-transform, all upgrades you have purchased carry over seamlessly. This allows you to manage your active state based on the current wave requirements without losing your financial investment.
