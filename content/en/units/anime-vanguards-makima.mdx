## Understanding Makima’s Unique Abilities

What makes the **Anime Vanguards Makima** unit truly "OP" is her complex kit that rewards tactical placement. She isn't just a damage dealer; she is a support powerhouse that buffs your entire field.

### Key Passive Skills
*   **Chains of Control:** Any unit placed before her becomes "Chained." This reduces the placement cost of those units by 10% and boosts their damage by 20%. Selling a chained unit triggers a bleed effect on enemies in range.
*   **Tied by Desire:** When a chained ally attacks a bleeding enemy, they inflict the "Wounded" status. Additionally, if the ally is a Demon Hunter, they receive a 30% damage boost.
*   **Bark for Me:** Every basic attack performed by a chained unit allows Makima to perform a follow-up attack, slowing enemies by 50% for 5 seconds.

## Strategic Placement and Team Synergy

To get the most out of your **Anime Vanguards Makima**, you need to build your team around her "Chained" mechanic. Because she rewards you for placing units *before* her, she works best as a mid-to-late game placement.

### Recommended Team Composition Strategy
1.  **Early Game:** Place your primary damage dealers and support units (like Rukia for buffs).
2.  **Mid Game:** Deploy the Demon Leader to chain your existing units. 
3.  **Late Game:** Use her "Say This Name" active ability to nuke the strongest boss on the map for 1,000% damage.

According to player experience, the ability to delete a chained ally to trigger massive burst damage makes her an incredible "boss killer." However, be careful—once a unit is deleted by this ability, it cannot be replaced. Use this nuke only when the situation is dire or to finish off a high-HP boss.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Is Anime Vanguards Makima worth the grind?
Absolutely. Her ability to provide cost reduction and massive damage buffs to chained allies makes her one of the most versatile units in the current meta.

### What is the best trait for Makima?
Community experts often recommend "Monarch" for the best damage scaling. However, if you are looking for cost-efficiency, "Jack of All Trades" is an excellent secondary option due to its cost-reduction benefits.

### Can I use Makima's active ability multiple times?
The "Say This Name" ability has a 20-second global cooldown. It is a powerful tool, but it should be used sparingly because it permanently removes one of your placed units from the field.

### How do I get the Nurse skin for Makima?
The exclusive "Nurse" skin is available through limited-time bundles. Keep an eye on the in-game shop during the Chainsaw Man event to see if it rotates back into availability.
