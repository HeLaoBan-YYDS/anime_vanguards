## Step-by-Step Evolution Questline

The journey to unlock this unit is split into several distinct phases. According to recent community reports, the most challenging part of the quest is the final boss rush stage, which requires perfect timing and unit management.

### Phase 1: Locating the Sun
1. **Initiate the Quest:** Check your Special tab to find the "Elastic Captain Vanguard" quest.
2. **Defeat the Bounty:** Clear any singular bounty stage to earn the "Compass Pose" familiar.
3. **Equip the Familiar:** Equip the Compass Pose onto Nami to trigger the next phase.
4. **Find the Map:** Head to Flower Country and look for an act with a golden lining. You have a 10% chance to drop the Treasure Map from this stage.
5. **The Dig:** Purchase the Item Finder from the Gold Shop. Use it on Stage 2, Act 4 (Sosora Shell) to locate the dig site and claim the Left Half of the Sun.

### Phase 2: The Pirate Dynasty and the Drums
Once you have the first piece, travel to the Pirate Dynasty. You must capture four points to spawn Zoro. After defeating him, allow Kaido to defeat you to receive the Drumstick item. This is a scripted event, so don't panic when your health drops to zero!
