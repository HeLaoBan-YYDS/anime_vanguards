## The New Vanguard Rarity: Sukuna and Gojo Overview

The Summer Update has introduced a brand-new rarity tier known as "Vanguard." This tier currently features two of the most iconic characters from the Jujutsu Kaisen universe: Sukuna (The King of Curses) and Gojo (The Strongest). Unlike previous legendary or mythical units, these Vanguard units come with unique "Clash" animations and incredibly deep passive trees.

## Analyzing the Summer Update Game Mode

The new game mode, set in a meticulously designed Shibuya map, introduces a zombie-survival twist to the standard tower defense formula. ## Deep Dive into Sukuna: The King of Curses

Sukuna is perhaps the most complex unit ever added to the game. His "History's Strongest" passive allows him to inflict both Burn and Bleed. When an enemy is already bleeding from his "Cleave" ability, his attacks upgrade to "Dismantle," which significantly buffs the damage over time and allows the DOT to bypass enemy shields and damage reduction.

One of Sukuna's most unique features is the "Binding Vow" system. Upon reaching certain upgrade levels, players can choose a specific vow that alters Sukuna's stats in exchange for a drawback. This allows players to tailor Sukuna to their specific needs, whether that means map-wide buffs or specialized boss killing.

### Sukuna's Binding Vows and Effects

| Binding Vow | Benefit | Trade-off |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Amalgamation** | Full map-wide immunity + 30% Damage | -50% Personal Damage |
| **Divine General** | Spawns Maharaga to walk the track | -30% Personal Damage |

Community reports indicate that spawning the Divine General (Maharaga) is one of the most visually impressive abilities in the game, though it requires careful management of Sukuna's placement to ensure the General's HP (which scales with Sukuna's damage) remains high enough to block enemies.

## Deep Dive into Gojo: The Inheritor of the Six Eyes

Gojo serves as the ultimate utility and DPS hybrid among the **anime vanguards units**. His "Void" ability is a 15-second time stop that affects every enemy in his massive range. While Void is active, Gojo receives a 100% damage buff, making it the perfect window to burn down a wave 40 or 50 boss.

Gojo also utilizes an "Energy Meter" system. Players can set the "Cursed Output" percentage per attack. For example, setting the output to 11% is a popular community strategy, as it allows for a steady stream of high-damage attacks without immediately depleting the meter and forcing a 3-second recharge window.

### Gojo's Passives and Utility

*   **Infinity:** Automatically slows enemies by 50% when they enter his range. If an enemy gets too close (within 20% of his range), they are repulsed backward.
*   **Six Eyes:** This passive makes Gojo immune to all weather effects and status debuffs, ensuring he never stops attacking even in the harshest environment maps.
*   **Energy Management:** By spending meter, Gojo buffs his critical rate and critical damage. If the meter hits zero, he enters a brief cooldown before refilling to max.

## Portals, Familiars, and Summer Capsules

The update isn't just about the units on the banner. Players can also participate in "Scorching Fate" trials by obtaining portals from the Esganor NPC in the new game mode. These portals are the primary way to earn "Summer Capsules," which contain rare Vanguard Familiars.

Familiars are a new equipment system that provides massive stat boosts to your units. For example, the Esganor Familiar can provide a 200% damage boost, though it has an incredibly low drop rate (reportedly 0.1% from capsules).

## Essential Tips for Managing Your Anime Vanguards Units

With the introduction of so many new mechanics, managing your collection of **anime vanguards units** can be daunting. Here are a few actionable tips based on high-level player experiences:

*   **Watch the UI:** The new Shibuya mode has many pop-ups. Players suggest turning off "Unit Cutscenes" and "Summon Models" in the settings to reduce lag and improve visibility during the chaotic "Showdown" waves.
*   **Trait Priority:** For Vanguard units like Sukuna and Gojo, the "Divinity" and "Monarch" traits remain the gold standard. Divinity, in particular, synergizes well with Gojo's Void ability, further increasing damage against time-stopped targets.
*   **Don't Sell Early:** Both Sukuna and Gojo have a passive where, if sold, they are "replaced as themselves." This means they return to your inventory with their upgrades reset, allowing for strategic repositioning without losing the unit for the rest of the match.
*   **Status Synergy:** Use units that inflict "Stone" (like King) alongside Sukuna. If a stoned enemy is hit by Sukuna's Cleave, the stun ends early but deals 50% more damage, and Sukuna receives a 50% damage buff for 30 seconds.

## FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

### Which is better: Sukuna or Gojo?
In the current meta for **anime vanguards units**, Gojo is generally considered better for general content due to his 15-second full-map time stop. However, Sukuna is superior for Infinite Mode and Boss Events where his stacking "Dismantle" DOT can bypass massive damage reduction stats.

### How do I get Vanguard Familiars?
Vanguard Familiars are obtained through Summer Capsules. These capsules are dropped from Scorching Fate portals, which you can earn by playing the new Summer game mode and speaking with the Esganor NPC in the subway zone.

### Can I evolve Sukuna or Gojo?
According to current player reports and the update log, the new Vanguard rarity units are not evolvable. They are considered "complete" units upon being pulled from the banner, though they can still be significantly improved through Traits and Memorialia.

### What is the "Stone" status effect?
Stone is a new debuff introduced with certain **anime vanguards units** like King. It stuns enemies for a set duration. If an enemy is hit while stoned, the stun ends early, but the attack deals 50% bonus damage. This creates a high-skill ceiling for timing your strongest attacks.
