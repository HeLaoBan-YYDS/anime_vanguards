## The Mechanics of Pity in Anime Vanguards

In the world of Anime Vanguards, the "pity" system is a safety net designed to protect players from extreme bouts of bad luck. Without a pity system, a player could theoretically pull thousands of times without ever seeing a Mythic unit. The system tracks your consecutive unsuccessful pulls and eventually forces a high-rarity drop once a certain threshold is met.

There are generally two types of pity players discuss: "Soft Pity" and "Hard Pity." Soft pity typically begins when your odds of pulling a high-rarity unit start to increase incrementally with every failed pull. Hard pity is the absolute limit—the point where the game guarantees the featured unit regardless of previous luck. According to community-verified data, the hard pity for a featured unit often sits around 200 pulls, which equates to roughly 9,000 gems depending on active discounts or banner specifics.
