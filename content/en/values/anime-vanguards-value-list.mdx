## Understanding the Anime Vanguards Economy

The economy in Anime Vanguards is dynamic, often shifting with every new update or balance patch. Unlike fixed-price shops, the trading plaza operates on the principles of supply and demand. According to player experience and community reports, three main factors determine a unit's worth: Value, Demand, and Liquidity.

Value is the numerical estimate of what a unit is worth in the current market. Demand represents how many players are actively seeking that unit, while Liquidity describes how quickly you can expect to sell or trade the unit for its full value. For instance, a unit with high value but low liquidity might take days to trade, whereas a "Liquid" unit can be moved almost instantly.
