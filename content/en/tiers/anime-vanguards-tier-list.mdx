## Strategic Tips for Burn-Based Compositions

The current meta heavily favors fire-based synergy. By stacking burn-applying units, you enable "detonation" mechanics that bypass enemy damage resistance. This is particularly effective against high-HP enemies in the late-game stages.

### Effective Team Building Tips

*   **Avoid Overlapping Debuffs:** Ensure your support units are not clearing burn stacks before your main DPS units can detonate them.
*   **Prioritize Placement:** Use units like Escanor for solo-carry scenarios where you want to minimize unit count and maximize individual scaling.
*   **Cycle Attacks:** For units like Quetzalcoatl, ensure they are cycling through their attack patterns to maintain the 20% fire ally damage buff.
*   **Invest in Memorias:** While the latest showcases were done without them, equipping the correct memoria can push your DPS numbers into the multi-million range.
